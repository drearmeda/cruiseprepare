Who we’re packing for
Cruise packing tips (from the travel agency):
Days on ship or ashore: casual — beachwear, shorts, sundress.
Formal evenings: tuxedo, dark suit, or dinner jacket for gentlemen; cocktail dress or gown for ladies.
Casual nights: slacks & shirt / casual dresses. No shorts or tank tops at dinner.
Carry-on: keep the quinceañera dresses, tuxedos, medication, cameras, and jewelry in a garment/carry-on bag — never checked luggage.
Pool & beach towels are provided on board — no need to pack your own.
Port days: always check the current all-aboard time at each port and stay on ship's time.
